Ayur Vaidya Pro - Disease Prediction & Ayurvedic Solutions
1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose
To create an intelligent system that predicts diseases based on patient symptoms and recommends appropriate Ayurvedic treatments and remedies.
1.2 Scope
The system will include symptom analysis, disease prediction, Ayurvedic treatment recommendations, and lifestyle suggestions.
